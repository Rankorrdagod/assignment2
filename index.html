Hi! My name is Evan and I'm not 100% sure what I want to put here... I'm sure like some people or maybe most people I don't like to talk about myself so I'll try and keep it short if not sweet.

I am a graduate of SAIT's GCPT program (Graphic Communications and Print Technologies) and am currently enrolled in the Web Dev course through SAIT now, which explains this website I suppose. After a few years in the print industry and an unfortunate illness I have decided to switch paths and focus on the web; because lets be honest, print, while it does have its purposes is more limited in its uses.

Prior to my time at SAIT I spent a lot of time in the sales area of different companies/industries including: PostMedia (Advertising Sales), Accurate Screen and Grating (manufacturing and distribution sales) and while in school as a Business Develpoment Agent for Dodge in Airdrie.

I have a cat, this is her. Her name's Rain and she loves Temptations "tempties".

Other than that I enjoy drawing ocasionally and playing video games in my spare time. Also I recently got into painting Warhammer, have yet to play! Check out the buttons at the bottom of the page for some coding stuff, or check the links at the top for some other shizzz.
